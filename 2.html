Seulgi


Seulgi is a member of Red Velvet. She is the main dancer in the group, and is recognized by everyone for her excellent dancing skills. She also plays the lead vocal position in the group and has excellent singing ability. She was born on February 10, 1994 in Ansan, Gyeonggi-do.
